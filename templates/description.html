FootballStats

Football-money is an application that allows you to analyze football player prices and perform currency conversion. With FootballStats, you can:

Find the Most Expensive Football Player

Football-money enables you to quickly find the most expensive football player on the market. Simply enter the player's name or club, and the application will provide the current value.

Convert Currencies

Football-money also allows you to convert the value of money between different currencies within a specified period.
